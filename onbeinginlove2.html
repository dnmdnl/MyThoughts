On Being In Love.

"Grow as We Go"
I want to explain something,
something only a few people know.
Though I won't have time to tell you everything,
I guess we just have to grow as we go.
I dont love you for your beauty.
I dont love you for your smile.
I love you for the sake of love,
Even if it means I wont have it in return.


"Purpose"
I want you to understand,
I do not mean to be annoying or to be a brat.
It's just that I've never had someone make me feel this way before.
And I know that this time it's real.
Is it too much to ask to see you everyday?
or to at least talk to you even if our mouths couldn't find a way?
I guess that's the purpose why in this book I stayed.
I could read it over and over again.
